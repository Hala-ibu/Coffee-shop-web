About us

Between the pages of a book and the rim of a coffee cup, reality takes a well-deserved break

At SUNSHINE, we strive to create a community hub for everyone who visits. Inspired by our love for both coffee and books, we set out to design a unique space where guests can indulge in expertly crafted brews while exploring our carefully curated library.

Each book on our shelves has been hand-picked, and we regularly update our collection with fresh titles. Customers can even contribute to the library, sharing their favourite reads with the community.

We also believe in giving back, with a donations program that lets customers support local literacy initiatives with either books or monetary contributions.
