ABOUT US:-

At Cozy Bean, we strive to create a community hub for everyone who visits. Inspired by our love for both coffee and books, we set out to design a unique space where guests can indulge in expertly crafted brews while exploring our carefully curated library. Each book on our shelves has been handpicked, and we regularly update our collection with fresh titles. Customers can even contribute to the library, sharing their favorite reads with the community. We’re passionate about coffee, and it shows in every cup we serve. From sourcing high-quality beans to crafting our drinks, we aim to deliver an exceptional experience. We also believe in giving back, with a donations program that lets customers support local literacy initiatives with either books or monetary contributions. Cozy Bean isn’t just a coffee shop; it’s a space to gather, connect, and enjoy life’s simple pleasures.



OWNER

At the heart of Cozy Bean Coffee & Library is a passionate owner with a love for coffee, books, and community. Driven by the vision of creating a cozy, welcoming space, they have poured their energy into every detail, from the vintage decor to the carefully curated book selection. With a background in Information Technology, they bring a modern touch to the cafe, integrating creative ideas for digital engagement, book tracking, and more. They believe in the power of a shared space to foster connection, knowledge, and relaxation. Whether you’re here to grab a quick coffee, dive into a novel, or chat with a fellow book lover, the owner’s mission is to make every visit feel like a warm, friendly experience. Always open to suggestions, they encourage visitors to help shape the space, whether through book recommendations, drink ideas, or community events. Stop by and say hello—the owner loves a good conversation and is always eager to share stories, whether about the latest bestseller or the perfect coffee brew.
