ABOUT US:

At SUNSHINE, we strive to create a community hub for everyone who visits. Inspired by our love for both coffee and books, we set out to design a unique space where guests can indulge in expertly crafted brews while exploring our carefully curated library. Each book on our shelves has been handpicked, and we regularly update our collection with fresh titles. Customers can even contribute to the library, sharing their favorite reads with the community. We’re passionate about coffee, and it shows in every cup we serve. From sourcing high-quality beans to crafting our drinks, we aim to deliver an exceptional experience. We also believe in giving back, with a donations program that lets customers support local literacy initiatives with either books or monetary contributions. Cozy Bean isn’t just a coffee shop; it’s a space to gather, connect, and enjoy life’s simple pleasures.
